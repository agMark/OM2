Summary The section provides repair information for front section of the fuselage frame. This portion of the fuselage frame contains heat treated elements, so a replacement of the entire section is typically necessary when significant damage occurs. This repair should only be performed by an Air Tractor authorized repair facility. These repairs are approved per Air Tractor Dwg. 12138.
