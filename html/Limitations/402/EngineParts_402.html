The Pratt & Whitney PT6A engine has several life limited components. P&W Service Bulletin XXXXX Revision No. XX, or later revision, describes the life limited components, and their limits for the PT6A-11AG series engines. Service Bulletin XXXXX Revision No. XX, or later revision, describes the life limited components, and their limits for the PT6A-15AG series engines. Service Bulletin XXXXX Revision No. XX, or later revision, describes the life limited components, and their limits for the PT6A-34AG series engines. As outline in these service bulletins operators are required to keep records of accumulated total cycles and all starts and flights for their engines to determine when the service life of life limited components has expired.

Engine dash numbers have been changed and service bulletin numbers and revisions should be updated.
