Extra-large Fowler flaps are incorporated on the Air Tractor. The flaps are electrically operated and may be stopped at any position from 0° to the maximum of 28° travel. The flaps are controlled by a switch conveniently mounted just below the throttle quadrant. The flaps have external markings which may be viewed from the cockpit with each mark indicating 10° of travel.

The flaps are very effective for both takeoff and landing. 10° of flap deflection is typically used for short takeoff rolls. The flaps are also useful during turns, although they are generally extended less than 10°.
