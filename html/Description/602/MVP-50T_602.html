The Electronics International MVP-50T Engine Monitor is an optional installation and replaces the following instruments:

  • ITT Gauge
  • Torquemeter
  • Gas Generator Tachometer (Ng)
  • Propeller Tachometer (Np)
  • Fuel Pressure Gauge
  • Oil Pressure Gauge
  • Fuel Quantity Gauge
  • Voltammeter

The following indicator lights are also incorporated into the system:

  • Chip Detector
  • Beta
  • Air Filter
  • Fuel Filter
