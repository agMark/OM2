Spring type main and tail landing gear are used on the Air Tractor airplanes. This type of landing gear provides a minimum of maintenance, low drag and considerable energy absorption for hard landings. In addition, a smooth ride is provided for operations from rough strips.

The main gear axles are made of heat-treated 4130N steel which are cadmium plated for corrosion protection. Tapered aluminum shims are used between the axle and main gear leg to provide proper camber and toe-in. The main gear wheels are Cleveland p/n 40-403 with a 29.00 x 11.00 10-ply rating tire installed. Right-hand and left-hand brakes are identical Cleveland p/n 30-231 units. Main wheel tire inflation pressure is 50 psi (unloaded) or 52 psi (loaded).

The tailwheel is a Cleveland 40-140C. A grease fitting provided in the side of the wheel is used to lubricate the bearings. The tailwheel tire is 17.5 x 6.25 10-ply rating. Tire pressure is 60 psi loaded or unloaded.

The tailwheel fork is of welded 4130N tubing construction and is heat-treated for strength. A bronze bushing and a tapered roller bearing are incorporated where the fork enters the tail wheel housing.

A tailwheel locking mechanism is provided and the tailwheel may be unlocked by pushing the control stick forward or by operating the manual tailwheel lock lever. This lifts the stainless locking pin out of the tailwheel fork and allows the tailwheel assembly to swivel 360°.

The original tailwheel lock mechanism is adjusted by loosening the four screws that attach the lock pin housing to the fork housing plate. The SureLock mechanism is adjusted using the adjustment bolts at the front and back of the housing. These mechanisms are interchangeable. The SureLock mechanism may be retrofit to all airplanes.

The brake cylinders are Cleveland p/n 10-23A. Brake fluid is conventional red petroleum-based Mil-H-5606A fluid. The brake fluid reservoir is Volkswagen p/n 411611311A and is mounted on top of the lower instrument panel where the fluid level is readily visible. The parking brake valves are Scott p/n 4500A-2. Stratoflex hoses are used from the pressure side of the master cylinder to the parking brake valve and stainless steel lines are routed from the valve to the bulkhead fitting of the master cylinder.The parking brake valve and stainless steel lines are then routed from the valve to the bulkhead fitting next to the main landing gear.
