The fuel gauges indicate the fuel quantity in each of the wing tanks. The input signal to the gauges allows the reading of fuel quantity in both the left-hand and right-hand wing tanks. The gauges do not display total fuel quantity.

Each fuel gauge is a microammeter that displays amperage signals from potentiometers attached to float arms in the fuel tanks. The faces of the gauges are calibrated in divisions which show fuel quantity in fractions of the FULL reading.
