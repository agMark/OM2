The exhaust pipes are Air Tractor p/n 51238-1 (left-hand) and 51238-2 (right-hand). Optional longer exhaust pipes are p/n 51238-7 (left-hand) and 51238-6 (right-hand). These pipes are attached with stainless MS20034-1 bolts, MS20500-428 nuts, and AN960C416 washers. There are exhaust covers provided with each aircraft and these covers should be installed after the engine has cooled.
