The prop installed on standard and optional engines is the 111" diameter, five-bladed, constant-speed Hartzell HC-B5MP-3C/M10876ANS propeller with reversing capability. The primary propeller governor is furnished with the engine. The overspeed governor is Woodward p/n A210507 for the -60AG or -45R engine. The overspeed governor takes over automatically in case of primary governor failure and reduces prop speed 30-60 RPM below the maximum of 1700 RPM.
