The horizontal stabilizers are all-metal and strut-supported for rigidity. The struts are constructed of streamlined 4130N tubing. The struts have a stainless-steel clevis attachment to the stabilizer. The clevis is adjustable in order to rig the stabilizers properly with the wing, and in a straight line from side to side.

The vertical fin is fully cantilevered and is of all-metal construction. A wire deflector cable is attached to the top of the vertical fin, and anchored to the top of the canopy.

The control surface hinge bearings have a single stainless-steel ball with a Teflon lining. Long bearing life can be expected without lubrication.

The elevator trim tabs act as servo tabs in addition to providing longitudinal trim for the Air Tractor. Because of the large tab size, the trim tabs provide a considerable amount of trim authority. Removable bronze bushings are provided at each joint. The bushings should be replaced as the wear so that trim tab free play is kept to a minimum. The all-metal rudder has a servo tab that also serves as an adjustable trim tab.
