The AT-602 is structurally certified to meet the requirements of FAR 23. The engine installation along with the systems and equipment meet FAR 23 requirements except in cases where inappropriate for agricultural operations. In these cases placards are installed on the instrument panel which prohibit flying under those special conditions. The AT-602 is certificated by the FAA for a takeoff gross weight of 12,500 pounds and a landing weight of 12,000 pounds.

Certification basis is FAR 23 with certain sections excepted as inappropriate for the special-purpose use of agricultural and forest and wildlife conservation.
