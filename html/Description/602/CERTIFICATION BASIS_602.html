The AT-602 is structurally certified to meet the requirements of FAR Part 23. The engine installation along with the systems and equipment meet FAR Part 23 requirements except in cases where inappropriate for agricultural operations. In such cases, placards are installed on the instrument panel which prohibits flying under those special conditions. The AT-602 is certificated by the FAA for a takeoff gross weight of 12,500 pounds and a landing weight of 12,000 pounds.

Certification basis is FAR Part 23 with certain sections excepted as inappropriate for the special-purpose use of agricultural, forest and wildlife conservation.
