Instruments provided with the basic airplane include a sensitive altimeter, airspeed indicator, ITT gauge, oil temperature gauge, oil pressure gauge, torquemeter, propeller tachometer, gas generator tachometer (Ng), compass, boom pressure gauge and fuel gauges.

All of the instruments are equipped with eyebrow lights for illumination in low-light conditions. The light intensity for both the upper and lower panels is controlled by the instrument light potentiometer.
