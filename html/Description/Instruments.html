Instruments provided with the basic airplane include a sensitive altimeter, airspeed indicator, ITT gauge, oil temperature gauge, oil pressure gauge, torque-meter, propeller tachometer, gas generator tachometer (Ng), compass, fuel gauges, and a volt-ammeter (or voltmeter).

All of the instruments are equipped with eyebrow lights for illumination in low-light conditions. The light intensity is controlled by the instrument-light potentiometer on both panels.
