The Electronics International MVP-50T Engine Monitor is optionally installed and replaces the following instruments: • ITT Gauge • Torquemeter • Gas Generator Tachometer (NG) • Propeller Tachometer (NP) • Fuel Pressure Gauge • Oil Pressure Gauge • Fuel Quantity Gauge • Voltammeter The following indicator lights are also incorporated into the system: • Chip Detector • Beta • Air Filter • Fuel Filter
