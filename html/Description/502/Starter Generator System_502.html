The starter-generator system produces and controls all electrical power for the aircraft. It works with the system's batteries and generator control unit (GCU) to power the electrical buses. All electrical subsystems receive their power from buses that are fed by the starter-generator system.

The AT-502/504 series aircraft are equipped with a 250-amp starter-generator and uses a QAD mounting kit.

The system is derated to a 150-amp system by using a 150-amp circuit breaker.
