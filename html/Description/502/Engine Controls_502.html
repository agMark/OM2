A quadrant on the left-hand side of the cockpit incorporates the Power lever, the Propeller control lever, and the Start control lever. The AT-504 has an additional power lever on the right-hand side of the cockpit. There are two types of quadrants: • Standard Air Tractor Quadrant • Kawak Quadrant These quadrants are described in the following sections. All engine controls utilize push-pull cable assemblies. These cable assemblies have very little friction and require no lubrication. See Figure 1-9 for the layout of engine controls.
