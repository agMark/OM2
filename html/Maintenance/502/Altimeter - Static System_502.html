The static port is contained located under the left hand wing while the static ports are located in the aft section of the fuselage spine.

CAUTION

Apply air pressure only to the line that leads to the pitot-static head. Do not apply air pressure to the line that leads to the instrument panel. Sensitive instruments may be damaged.

The static ports are the low points of the system and are normally self-draining, but there are plugged “T" fittings on the rear face of the static port buttons in the aft fuselage. These plugs may be removed to drain residual moisture in the static-system lines.

Pitot/Static Calibration

Using a pitot static tester, attach the positive pressure line to the pitot tube and the negative pressure line to the static port. Maintain compliance with part 91.411 by using Appendix E to FAR Part 43, which outlines the procedures for performing the altimeter system test and inspection for the transponder.
