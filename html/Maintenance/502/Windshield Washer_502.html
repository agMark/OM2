The wiring schematic for the windshield washer is shown in Figure 2-42 below. The pump motor starts when the switch is closed and the solenoid valve opens. The flow of windshield washing fluid will continue until the switch is released. The purpose of the solenoid is to provide a sharp cut-off of fluid when the switch is released. This prevents the dribbling of fluid onto the windshield.
