The spray pump is an Agrinautics p/n 65219 attached to an Agrinautics p/n 60130 strut assembly with AN5-13A bolts.

The standard fan is the Micronair p/n AT-4300 which is used for low to moderate spray volume. Other fans that are approved for use are the Arrowprop A2-AT4T34 four blade wood fan and the Weath-Aero F100AA-144 or F200-144. The AT-504 uses the Micronair or Weath-Aero fans.
