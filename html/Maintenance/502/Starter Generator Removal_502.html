To remove the starter generator:

  1. Disconnect cables.
  2. One mechanic should maintain the alignment of the starter-generator by holding the unit in position, keeping the starter generator mounting surfaces flush with the Quick Attach/Detach adapter (QAD pad). A second mechanic should loosen and remove the 'V' band clamp.
  3. Carefully remove the starter-generator from the QAD pad by maintaining spline alignment to prevent binding of the starter-generator driveshaft.
