The wiring schematic for the stall warning horn is shown in Figure 2-41 below. The horn is connected in series with the lift-detector switch that is located near the leading edge of a mid-span of the left-hand wing.

INSERT Figure 2-41 Wiring Schematic - Fuel/ Flaps/ Horn

The lift-detector switch is controlled by the position of a tab extending forward from the wing leading edge. The weight of the tab holds it down during lower angles of attack, allowing the switch to remain open. As the angle of attack increases, the tab eventually pivots upward, closing the switch and causing the stall warning horn to sound.
