As a general rule, if the Starter Generator functions normally in the Starting mode there will be nothing wrong with it that would prevent it from functioning in the Generate mode.

Before attempting to correct any problem with this system first read and understand Section 2.40.2 and acquaint yourself with the location and function of the various components. Look for obvious problems such as overheating, burned wires, loose components, and loose electrical connections. Disconnect the GCU cannon plug and inspect it and all other electrical connections for corrosion. Correct any of these problems before proceeding.

Equipment

Equipment recommended for troubleshooting the charging system is shown below:

  • A multimeter capable of .01V resolution in the 0-2.5V range, .15V resolution in the 0-30V range and capable of reading from a fraction of an ohm to several kilohms will be required.
