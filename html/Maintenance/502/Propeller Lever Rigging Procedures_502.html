Refer to P&W Maintenance Manual for appropriate engine for general instructions and drawings. Rig Prop Controls as follows:

  1. Position governor arm on governor against stop, as shown on drawing XXXXX.
  2. Position prop lever in cockpit at feather stop and adjust swivel ends and REB3N bearing so that governor arm is also against feather stop.
  3. With governor arm against high RPM stop prop lever in cockpit should be full forward with 1/16" minimum cushion at forward stop.
  4. Check operation of prop cable forward and aft for free movement. Lock all check nuts.
  5. Perform flight line engine run-up. Check feather position and high RPM setting of XXXX RPM. Adjust high RPM stop if required. Re-set 1/16" min. cushion on forward end of prop lever.
