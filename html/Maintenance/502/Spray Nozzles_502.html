Forty-one installed spray nozzles are standard from the factory. These are manufactured by Spraying Systems Inc. and have a diaphragm check valve, body and spray tip. A variety of orifice sizes are available in the Quick Vee Jet spray tips. The aircraft is equipped at the factory for medium volume applications. For high volume spraying an additional 40 nozzles may be installed. Be sure to apply 3M Permatex or equivalent to the threads of the hex nipples or plugs during installation.
