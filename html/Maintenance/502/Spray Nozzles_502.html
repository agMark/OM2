Spray nozzles are standard from the factory. These have a diaphragm check valve, body and spray tip. A variety of orifice sizes are available in the Quick Vee Jet spray tips. The aircraft is equipped at the factory for medium volume applications. For high volume spraying, additional nozzles may be installed.

Be sure to apply 3M Permatex or equivalent to the threads of the hex nipples or plugs during installation.
