Restraints are a vital piece of safety equipment. It is imperative that they are kept in good working condition in case of a crash. The aircraft must not be flown without restraints or with damaged restraints. The restraints are inoperable if any of the following criteria are met:

  1. The seatbelt airbag has a tear in airbag cover which exceeds .25 inches or has excessive fraying
  2. The buckle/belt is non-functioning
  3. A cable or component is disconnected while the aircraft is in service
  4. System components are outside of their service life

Check the restraints frequently, not just during the annual inspection. Damaged restraints wont preform as intended in a crash, compromising the pilots's satey.
