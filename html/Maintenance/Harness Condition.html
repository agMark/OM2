Restraints are a vital piece of safety equipment. It is imperative that they are kept in good working condition. The aircraft must not be flown without restraints or with damaged restraints. The restraints are inoperable if any of the following criteria are met:

  1. Excessive fraying (greater than 1/4 inch)
  2. Nicks or tears, especially near edges of adjacent parts
  3. Damaged stitching or cracked / torn fabric.
  4. Excessively faded color indicating deterioration due to ultraviolet light exposure
  5. The buckle/belt is non-functioning

Check the restraints frequently, not just during the annual inspection. Damaged restraints wont preform as intended in a crash, compromising the pilots's satey.
