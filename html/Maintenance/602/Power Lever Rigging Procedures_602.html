Rig Engine Power Controls and Reversing System as follows (for PT6A-60AG and PT6A-65AG):

  1. Disconnect reversing cable from propeller control cam on aft right-hand side of engine. Move the fork and cable assembly up, inboard and out of the way of the propeller control cam.
  2. Remove propeller reversing lever from forward end of push-pull cable assembly. Do not lose the bushing (P&W p/n 3011554) in the top of the arm. Install Hartzell p/n A-3044 carbon block in reversing arm.
  3. Install carbon block at end of reversing lever into the beta ring. Block should have .001 clearance. If there is not enough clearance, use fine sandpaper on a flat sanding surface, sanding a little off the block until .001 clearance is obtained.
  4. Install the new P&W p/n 3011554 bushing on top of the propeller reversing arm and install the bolt, nut and cotter pin. Check the inside surface of the beta valve clevis to see if it is flush with the forward end of the beta valve nut. If not, adjust the propeller governor interconnect rod until it is flush. The governor air bleed link arm must be against the stud. Torque jam nuts and safety with MS20995C-020 wire. Install nut, P&W p/n 30105566 washers each side of rod end bearing and cotter pin.
  5. With power lever at ground idle, adjust power cable to position control cam shaft on point of propeller control cam.
  6. Adjust FCU rod from propeller control cam for preliminary idle setting on FCU.
  7. Tighten all check nuts and rod ends. Safety wire where required and cotter pin all castle nuts.
  8. Make any other adjustments on the flightline after first run of engine.

Rig Engine Power Controls and Reversing System as follows (PT6A-45R only):

  1. Disconnect reversing cable from propeller control cam on aft right-hand side of engine. Move the fork and cable assembly up, inboard and out of the way of the propeller control cam.
  2. Remove propeller reversing lever from forward end of push-pull cable assembly. Do not lose the bushing (P&W p/n 3011554) in the top of the arm. Install Hartzell p/n A-3044 carbon block in reversing arm.
  3. Install carbon block at end of reversing lever into the beta ring. Block should have .001 clearance. If there is not enough clearance, use fine sandpaper on a flat sanding surface, sanding a little off the block until .001 clearance is obtained.
  4. Install the new P&W p/n 3011554 bushing on top of the propeller reversing arm and install the bolt, nut and cotter pin. Check the inside surface of the beta valve clevis to see if it is flush with the forward end of the beta valve nut. If not, adjust the propeller governor interconnect rod until it is flush. The governor air bleed link arm must be against the stud. Torque jam nuts and safety with MS20995C-020 wire. Install nut, P&W p/n 30105566 washers each side of rod end bearing and cotter pin.
  5. With power lever at ground idle position, adjust rear end of reversing cable fork where the clevis pin is free in the top hole of the propeller control cam. Then shorten fork 1/2 turn to take out any slack. Reinstall clevis pin, washer and cotter pin.
  6. With power lever in ground idle position, adjust the FCU rod to clear the FCU extension arm 1/8". Adjust each end as necessary, tighten check nuts on each end of FCU rod and install cotter pins.
  7. Tighten all check nuts and rod ends. Safety wire where required and cotter pin all castle nuts.
  8. Make any other adjustments on the flightline after first run of engine.
