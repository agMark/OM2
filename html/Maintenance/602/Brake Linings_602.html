There are six brake lining pairs on each brake. Three pairs are each located on the front and rear respectively. The pairs are located on either side of the brake disc directly beneath each of the six pistons.

The end pair of brake linings can be seen on the top and bottom ends of both sides of the brake disc while installed on the airplane. New brake linings are .218" thick. The brake linings should be replaced when the remaining thickness is less than .020" to prevent rivet damage to the brake disc.
