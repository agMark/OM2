Steel parts are to be cleaned of all oils and then sandblasted. If sandblasting is not possible, clean the part thoroughly, wipe dry, sand and etch with a mixture of 1.0 gallon of isopropyl alcohol and 1/2 fluid ounce of concentrated phosphoric acid. If applying finish coat over bare metal, prime first with High Solids Epoxy Primer (see above) and paint using Urethane Topcoat process (see above).
