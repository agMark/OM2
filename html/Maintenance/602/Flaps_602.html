The flaps are attached to 3/8" thick aluminum mid and inboard flap arms with AN4-24A bolts and to the outboard arm with an AN4-24A bolt. The order of assembly (bolt pointing inboard) is a AN970-4 large washer under the bolt head, bearing (Fafnir KP4), steel flap arm, AN960-416 washer and AN365-428A nut.

The flap pushrod is attached to the flap with a AN4-25A bolt pointing outboard, AN970-4 washer under the bolt head, AN960-416 washer, steel flap arm, AN960-416 washer, and AN365-428 nut. as shown in . The pushrod has an adjustable Fafnir RE4F6-2 bearing with an AN316-6 check nut. Be sure that the bearing is positioned to allow the pushrod to be rotated slightly from side to side.

During 12-month inspections (See ), the AN4-25A bolt through the pushrod should be checked for straightness, and if bent, both bolt and bearing should be immediately replaced. Remove the lower fuselage side skins under the cockpit at 100-hour intervals and check to see if pushrods drag on any fuselage parts while the flaps are being cycled through the full range of travel. With the flaps positioned full down and then full up, move the ailerons through full range of travel and check for clearance.
