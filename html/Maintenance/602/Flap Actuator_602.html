The actuator assembly has a threaded collar. This collar is threaded internally to match the threads on the worm drive threads of the actuator and externally to match the threads of the p/n 70037-1 Air Tractor Tee assembly. The Tee assembly is attached to two 1/4" aluminum 70905-1 arm assemblies bolted to the flap torque tube assembly. The Tee assembly has a steel grease shield and cap attached. The shield is p/n 70039-1 and is attached to the Tee with two AN500A5-3 screws and lock washers. Be sure the lock washers are under the head of the AN500A5-3 screws. The cap is a 1-1/8" SC Caplug attached to the shield with a TY-525 Tyrap.

The Tee assembly which screws into the threaded collar has a AN565 D6H4 set screw located on the top surface of the Tee. The threaded collar has a large diameter rounded surface, and just forward is a smaller diameter part on the collar used for tightening the collar against the Tee. Whenever it is necessary to remove the flap actuator, factory practice is to first loosen the set screw in the Tee assembly with a small allen wrench, placing a soft rag or duct tape on the small diameter of the threaded collar to prevent scratching the finish. Use water-pump or locking pliers to break loose the connection between the threaded collar and Tee assembly. The threaded collar may then be removed from the Tee by hand. Be careful to not dent or scratch the finish of the ball nut as small dents or defects will interfere with the action of the balls inside.
