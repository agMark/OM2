The rudder trim controls in the cockpit are shown in Fig Cross Reference. Rudder trim is controlled by a horizontal handwheel on the left-hand wall of the cockpit. This handwheel is connected via a pushrod to the Quadra-Stat sector level in the aft fuselage. The Quadra-Stat is a positioning sector which provides a friction lock at the position selected by the control lever. The Quadra-Stat is shown in . If the rudder trim slips in flight, the friction screws may be tightened to prevent further occurences.

The Quadra-Stat output arm is connected via a pushrod to the idler block positioned just below the horizontal stabilizer on the right-hand face of the fin rear spar. A rod attached to the arm of this idler progresses aft to the rudder tab attachments.
