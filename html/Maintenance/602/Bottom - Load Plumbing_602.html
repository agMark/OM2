Details of the bottom-load plumbing are shown below in Fig Cross Reference. The bottom-load valve is 3 inches in diameter and located on the left-hand side of the aircraft. The valve is Transland p/n 24446. The aft half of the bottom load plumbing is a 3-inch tube and is p/n 80662-4. The forward tube is p/n 80735-1.

Hoses are 80376-3 and 80376-1 with QS200M56S clamps. Clamps that attach the 80046-1 hose to the ON-OFF valve are QS200M28S.
