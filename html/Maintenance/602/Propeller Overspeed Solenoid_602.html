The propeller overspeed solenoid provides is used to test the functionality of the propeller overspeed governor which switches the hydraulic propeller-control circuit from variable RPM to fixed rpm in the event of primary governor failure. When activated, this circuit maintains propeller speed to 30-60 rpm below the engine maximum of XXXX rpm. The system is operated with a switch that is installed on the lower panel.
