Be sure and cover the exhaust ports and keep the propeller tethered when your airplane is left outside. The propeller needs to be tethered to prevent windmilling of the power section--the oil pump is in the rear of the engine and will not spin with the prop. Therefore, the pump will not provide oil to the front of the engine.

Don't be too embarrassed the first time you start the engine with the propeller tethered, I can assure you that you are not the first.
