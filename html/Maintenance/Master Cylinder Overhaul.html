The overhaul procedure is as follows:

  1. Remove retaining ring and remove piston assembly from housing.
  2. Remove piston nut (Item 11) and disassemble piston assembly. Replace all components listed and provided in kit.
  3. Reinstall all parts as shown above. Ensure 095-25100 (Item 10) is oriented properly. The sharp edge and rough surface should be towards the piston (Item 7)
  4. Torque nut (Item 14) to 35-40 in-lb.
  5. Reinstall piston assembly retaining the original orientation of the clevis. Install retaining ring.

Test uninstalled assembly:

  1. Blow low pressure, dry, clean shop air though the pressure port hole in the housing and observe air passing out through the inlet. This assures an unobstructed passageway between the washer seal and piston.
  2. With air still passing through port, depress shaft approximately 1/16‚Äù which should cut off air passing through the assembly and out the inlet demonstrating that the check valve is closing and sealing properly.
  3. Manually actuate the cylinder rod for a minimum of three full length strokes.
