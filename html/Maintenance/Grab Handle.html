An aluminum grab handle is provided on the top of the forward part of the cockpit on AT-802's.

Before 2015, the grab handle was secured with AN4 bolts. After that, the bolts were changed to stronger NAS1304 bolts to decrease the likelihood of bolt failure. If your grab handle is secured with AN4 bolts, it is recommended that you replace them with NAS1304 bolts.

Grab Handle Bolts

Also in 2015, the length of the threaded recess (bore) inside the 11509-1 grab handle was increased from .75 inches to 1.62 inches so that longer bolts could be used. This was done to increase the strength of the connection. If you replace the grab handle, it is recommended that you use the longer "Rev A" version so that you may also use the longer bolts.

Suggested bolt replacement intervals are shown in . Air Tractor suggests the following actions be taken depending on your airplane's configuration at the bolt's next replacement interval:

Grab Handle Bolt Action
11509-1 Pre 2015 AN4-22 Replace with grab handle with new 11509-1 with longer bore and use NAS1304-44 bolts.
11509-1 Pre 2015 NAS1304-30 Replace bolt with new NAS1304-30.
