Rig Engine Power Controls and Reversing System as follows:

  1. Disconnect reversing cable from propeller control cam on the aft right-hand side of engine. Move fork and cable assembly up & inboard, out of the way of the propeller control cam.
  2. Remove propeller reversing lever from forward end of push-pull cable assy. Do not lose the bushing in the top of the arm. Install Hartzell carbon block in reversing arm.
  3. Install carbon block at end of reversing lever into the beta ring. Block should have .001 clearance. If not, use fine sandpaper on flat sanding surface, sanding a little off block until .001 clearance is obtained.
  4. Install the new P&WC bushing on top of the propeller reversing arm and install bolt, nut and cotter pin. Check the inside surface of the beta valve clevis to see if it is flush with the forward end of the beta valve nut. If not, adjust propeller governor interconnect rod until it is flush. Governor air bleed link arm must be against stud. Torque jam nuts with .020" safety wire. Install nut and P&WC washers on each side of rod end bearing and cotter pin.
  5. With Power Lever at Ground Idle, adjust power cable to position control cam shaft on point of propeller control cam.
  6. Adjust FCU rod from propeller control cam for preliminary idle setting on FCU.
  7. Tighten all check nuts and rod ends. Safety wire where required and cotter pin all castle nuts.
  8. Make any other adjustments on flight line after first engine run.
