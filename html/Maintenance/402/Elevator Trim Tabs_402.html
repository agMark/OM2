Details of the elevator trim tabs are shown below in Fig Cross Reference. The elevator trim tabs are attached to the elevators with screws. There are NAS anchor nuts installed on the elevators for these screws.

The trim tabs are shimmed laterally with shims. Lateral free play should be between .050 and .020 in. Remove the machine screw to lubricate the tab bushings. Apply a general purpose oil to the bushings through the screw hole and at the end of the tab, replacing the oil hole plug screw after completion.
