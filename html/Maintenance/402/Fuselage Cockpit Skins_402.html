The cockpit metal skins are riveted to the floor and to the support angles with MS20470AD rivets. Attachments to the door frames are blind rivets. The cockpit skins may be removed by drilling out the rivets where necessary and is not a difficult operation. However, the hopper should first be removed in order to remove the cockpit floor.
