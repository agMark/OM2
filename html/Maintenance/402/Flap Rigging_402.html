With the flap actuator in the UP position, the flap pushrods are adjusted so that the lower side of the flaps form a straight line with the lower side of the fuel tank at station 61.0. Flap travel from this point is then set at 20° +/- 1.5°. Flap markings on the flap are located opposite the wing flap bay upper skin so that the pilot will view the markings at 10° and at full travel.
