If the microswitches function correctly, the flap motor may be checked as follows:

  1. Slide the conduit down from the wire splice (Wristlock) where the red and black wires go into the wire bundle.
  2. Note which wires the black wire from the motor connects to.
  3. Use an external 24-volt power source (protected with a circuit breaker) and attach the negative power source wire to the red flap motor wire and attach the positive power source wire through the circuit breaker to the black flap motor wire. The motor will rotate in one direction. Reverse the power source wire connections and verify that the motor now rotates in the opposite direction.
  4. If the motor on a C100168-4 actuator does not respond as described above, it is faulty and should be replaced. It is attached to the actuator by two 10-32 nuts. Be sure rubber coupling is in good condition
