The long pushrod assembly attached to the over-center controls of the gate box has a NMB Mil Spec bearing in the aft end and a Bunting bronze bushing in the forward end. The aft end connects to the torque tube assembly, which is supported on the fuselage lower longerons with two Fafnir bearings attach with AN bolts.

The boot attaches to the lower fuselage skin boot ring with screws and Mil Spec nuts. A pushrod connects the lower torque tube to the hopper handle in the cockpit. This pushrod has an adjustable Fafnir bearing on each end. The hopper handle is supported on each end with Fafnir bearings. AN bolts pass through the bearings.

Dump Handle Grip Inspection and Replacement

The grip should be replaced if it is found to be degraded or damaged or impinging on the release button.

To remove the grip, you will likely need to cut it lengthwise then peel. Depending on the existence and security of adhesive, scraping and sanding may be required. Once the old grip is completely removed, replace it as follows:

  1. Remove the red button by rotating counterclockwise. The button is retained with Loctite so it may be somewhat difficult.
  2. Apply 3M Interior Trim (08088) or equivalent spray adhesive and slide the new grip onto the handle until the top flange bottoms out against the handle
  3. Allow adhesive to cure for at least 15 minutes.
  4. Reinstall the release button with Loctite 242 (blue) ensuring that the button is not threaded too far. Button should be installed with a minimum of three full turns but not so far that it impinges on the handle or grip. Verify that there is at least 1/8" of clearance between the button and grip/handle when the release is activated.
