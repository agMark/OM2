When leaving your airplane outside, be sure and cover the exhaust ports and keep the propeller tethered. The propeller needs to be tethered to prevent the windmilling of the power section. The oil pump is in the rear of the engine and does not rotate when the propeller windmills. Therefore the pump will not provide oil to the front of the engine.
