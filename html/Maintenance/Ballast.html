It is possible to install ballast onto the engine mount to shift the center of gravity forward. Installation of ballast is to be performed in accordance with Air Tractor Drawing 52199. Contact Air Tractor Customer Service for ballast weights, installation kit and instructions.

For planning purposes, the weight of the ballast weights are shown in the following table:

Location Part Number Weight (lb)
LH 52284-1 73.2
RH 52292-1 41.96
TOP, AFT 52325-2 46.4
TOP, FWD 52501-2 126.5

All weights can be assumed to be located at FS -70.0. Individual weights may be installed or removed as needed to achieve the desired weight and balance. Total weight must not exceed 300 lb including lead that may be installed in the engine ring. 50821-70 and 50821-78 engine mounts installed on AT-802's (2-seaters) have lead in the engine ring. 50821-77 and 50821-79 engine mounts installed on AT-802A's do not have lead in the engine ring. Contact Air Tractor Customer Service if you are unsure.

After installing ballast weights, it is recommended that you re-weigh the aircraft to verify weight and balance.
