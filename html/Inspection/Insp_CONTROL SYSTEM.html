CS1 (100) Check rudder cables for wear at pulley locations and at entrance to fuselage (Ref SL77). Check to see if cotter pin guard is installed per Service Letter #200.
CS2 (100) Check tail wheel lock cable for wear at pulley and at entrance to fuselage. Check spring on end of lock cable for wear.
CS3 (100) Check cockpit controls for end-play or slack.
CS4 (100) Check for missing neoprene washers at aileron and elevator stops.
CS5 (100) Check all trim/boost tabs for excessive free play.
CS6 (100) Inspect flap torque tube for cracks, proper attachment, bearing condition.
CS7 (100) Check all flight controls are free and correct with no chafing, jamming, or binding through all possible combinations of control positions.
CS8 (100) Check elevator down spring in control system in aft fuselage for breakage or wear in spring ends.
CS9 (100) Inspect upper and lower aileron torque tubes for cracks, proper attachment, lubrication and bearing condition.
CS101 (300) Check all elevator pushrods and bellcranks for bearing condition, clearance to other parts, loose check nuts, end-play on bellcranks, wobble in idlers.
CS201 (2000) Inspect rudder pedals per Cross Reference Placeholder.
CS501 (12 Months) Check for wear in the aluminum rudder adjustment channel.
CS502 (12 Months) Check rudder pedal attach brackets and tube for cracks. (Ref SL395, SL345, SL329)
CS503 (12 Months) Check all aileron push-rods and bellcranks for bearing condition, clearance to other parts, loose check nuts, end-play in bellcranks and weld condition. Weld beads on the bellcrank support assembly should run the entire length.
CS504 (12 Months) Check elevator controls friction device for proper adjustment as described in . Check the diameter of the friction device wear ring on the elevator control idler. The minimum diameter is 1.0 inch. Replace the idler if wear is excessive. (Aircraft s/n 802-0376/-4004 and subs and aircraft modified in accordance with Service Information Letter #802-0090 do not have the Friction Damper installed and this inspection does not apply).
CS505 (12 Months) Perform rudder control system inspection per Service Letter #317.
